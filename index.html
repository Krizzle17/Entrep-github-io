Nina Ellaine Dizon

Founder & CEO of Colourette Cosmetics

About Nina

Nina started her entrepreneurial journey by selling secondhand clothes online and eventually ventured into skincare and beauty. She founded Colourette Cosmetics in 2015 to offer affordable, inclusive, and high-quality makeup for Filipinas.

Milestones

2013: Started selling soaps and makeup online.
2015: Launched Colourette Cosmetics.
2020: Leveraged TikTok to grow Colourette's community.
Present: Runs Colourette and Fresh Formula, and advocates for social issues.

Nina’s Quality Trait as an Entrepreneur

Risk-Taking

  • She risked dropping out of college to fully commit to her business journey.

Creativity

  • Her branding and product development show a deep understanding of what the market needs, especially for Filipinas.

Resourcefulness

  • With limited capital, she used social media and digital tools to grow her brand organically.

Authenticity

  • Nina stays real and transparent with her audience and consumers, which built trust and a loyal community.

Consumer-Centered Mindset

  • Nina listens actively to customer feedback. She values her customers’ voices and adapts her business to meet their needs.
Her Motivations for Pursuing Entrepreneurship

Desire for Financial Independence

  • Coming from a modest background, she wanted to build something of her own to support herself and her family.

Passion for Beauty & Empowerment

  • She noticed the lack of inclusive products in the local beauty market and wanted to make a difference.

Creating Impact

  • Beyond profits, Nina is driven by the desire to uplift brown-skinned Filipinas and advocate for social awareness and empowerment.

CONCLUSION

Nina Ellaine Dizon is a true example of modern Filipino entrepreneurship. From humble beginnings to building a well-loved brand like Colourette Cosmetics, she proves that with passion, grit, and innovation, success is achievable. Her story continues to inspire young entrepreneurs, especially women, to chase their dreams and make a meaningful impact in both business and society.
